Features

All the features you want. Raining Vegetables makes it easy to track your money and farming activities for your farm.

Sales reports

A sales report helps you understand which activities have the highest and lowest returns for your farming business.

Expense reports

Get insight into your farm's expenses. See which activities are making you lose money so you can manage these expenses for future gains.

Productivity reports

See which farming practices aided in higher yields and which ones did not so you can course correct for future yields.

Budget planning

Set a budget plan and track your expenditures to monitor how your current expenditure aligns with your budget goals.

What you get.

Track all your farming sectors

Grow 3 different crops and rear animals? We got you. Keep track of all activities in your different farming sectors by entering farm data into the app. Your records will be kept securely so that you can access all farm records at any time and from any location.

Track your finances

Find information about your farm's financial state by viewing your revenue, expenses, and budget limits in one place to help you keep track of your farm's financials.

Keep track of your productivity records

Find a collection of all your farm records, broken down into different categories for easy tracking. A breakdown of your harvest, planting, labor, and all general records, among other things.
